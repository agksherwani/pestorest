Professional Earwig Treatment in Toronto

Earwigs are a seasonal nuisance across Toronto, appearing in homes from late spring through early fall. These nocturnal insects with their distinctive rear pincers are primarily outdoor creatures that live in moist soil, under mulch, beneath landscape stones, and in garden debris. They become a household pest when they migrate indoors seeking moisture during hot, dry spells or shelter during heavy rain. In Toronto, garden-level units, basement apartments, and townhomes with direct garden access are most frequently affected, particularly in neighbourhoods with lush gardens and mature landscaping like Rosedale, Lawrence Park, The Beaches, and the Danforth.

PestoRest Pest Control eliminates earwig invasions with a two-pronged approach: exterior perimeter treatment to create a barrier that kills earwigs before they enter, and interior treatment for earwigs already inside your home. We also provide specific habitat modification recommendations that make your property less attractive to earwigs, reducing the pressure on your home from the surrounding outdoor population.

Why Earwigs Invade Toronto Homes

Earwigs need moisture to survive, which is why they are most commonly found in kitchens, bathrooms, laundry rooms, and basements. Toronto's summer weather patterns create conditions that drive earwigs indoors: extended hot, dry periods send them seeking moisture, while heavy rainstorms flush them out of their outdoor hiding spots. Homes with gardens directly adjacent to the foundation, thick mulch beds against the house, or dense groundcover provide a bridge between outdoor earwig habitat and interior entry points.

In Toronto's townhome complexes across North York, Scarborough, and Etobicoke, shared garden areas and close foundation-to-garden proximity make earwig entry almost inevitable without prevention. Garden-level apartments in Riverdale, Leslieville, and The Junction are similarly vulnerable, with window wells serving as both collection points and entry corridors for earwigs moving toward the building's foundation.

Our Treatment Approach

PestoRest's earwig treatment creates a perimeter barrier around your Toronto home using a residual insecticide applied to the foundation wall, along door thresholds, around window frames, and in window wells. This barrier intercepts earwigs as they approach the building envelope. Inside, we treat along baseboards, around bathroom and kitchen plumbing, and in any areas where earwigs have been observed. A granular bait may also be applied to garden beds immediately adjacent to the foundation to reduce the outdoor population that puts pressure on your home.

Toronto Earwig Fact

European earwigs are the most common species in Toronto. Despite their intimidating pincers, they are harmless to humans. They are, however, voracious feeders on garden plants and can damage seedlings, marigolds, dahlias, and lettuce. Professional perimeter treatment protects both your home and your garden.
