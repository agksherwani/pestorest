Our Story

Born in Kitchener, Built for the Waterloo Region

PestoRest Pest Control was founded in 2020 by a team of licensed pest management professionals who saw a gap in the Kitchener-Waterloo market. Too many homeowners were dealing with slow response times, hidden fees, and companies that treated every pest the same way.

We started with a simple idea: provide fast, transparent, and science-backed pest control that is tailored to the specific conditions of Southwestern Ontario. From the older homes in Victoria Hills to new builds in Doon South, from student housing near the universities to commercial kitchens on King Street, every property has different vulnerabilities. We built our protocols around that reality.

In just a few years, we have grown from a two-person operation to a full team of licensed technicians serving Kitchener, Waterloo, Cambridge, Guelph, Brantford, Elmira, Stratford, and New Hamburg. We have protected over 2,400 homes, maintained a 4.9-star Google rating from 214 reviews, and earned CPMA certification that demonstrates our commitment to professional standards.

Every technician on our team is Ontario-licensed, fully insured, and trained in Integrated Pest Management. We use Health Canada-approved products, eco-friendly methods where possible, and always provide a written guarantee with every treatment.

Our Mission

Pest to Rest: Because Your Home Deserves Peace

Our mission is to deliver fast, effective, and discreet pest control that gives Kitchener-Waterloo homeowners and business operators peace of mind. We believe that dealing with pests should never be stressful, embarrassing, or confusing. From the first call to the final follow-up, we make the process simple, transparent, and guaranteed.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Three principles guide every decision we make and every treatment we perform.

Integrity First

We never upsell unnecessary treatments. Every recommendation is based on a thorough inspection of your specific situation. If we do not find a pest problem, we will tell you honestly and you will not pay a cent.

Responsible Methods

We prioritize Integrated Pest Management, using the least invasive methods first. All products are Health Canada-approved and applied by licensed professionals. We offer eco-friendly and non-chemical alternatives whenever they are effective.

Community Commitment

We live and work in Kitchener-Waterloo. Our technicians know these neighbourhoods, understand the local pest pressures, and treat every home as if it were their own. We support local businesses and give back to the communities we serve.

Our Team

Licensed Professionals, Local Experts

Our team of 14 licensed technicians brings decades of combined experience in structural pest management, wildlife biology, and entomology. Every team member holds an Ontario Exterminator Licence and participates in ongoing training to stay current with the latest pest control methods, products, and regulations.

We invest heavily in our people because we know that the quality of our service depends entirely on the skill and professionalism of the person who shows up at your door. Our technicians are trained not just in pest elimination, but in customer communication, safety protocols, and respectful property care.

From our dispatchers who answer your first call to the technicians who perform the treatment and the managers who follow up afterward, every member of the PestoRest team is committed to delivering the best pest control experience in the Waterloo Region.
