Why Stratford Properties Need Specialized Pest Control

Stratford is defined by two things that directly impact its pest landscape: stunning Victorian architecture and a world-renowned theatre festival that draws hundreds of thousands of visitors annually. The festival season runs from April through November, filling Stratford’s hotels, bed-and-breakfasts, and vacation rentals with guests from across the globe. This high-volume, high-turnover hospitality environment makes bed bugs Stratford’s most commercially damaging pest — a single infestation can result in cancelled bookings, negative reviews, and thousands in lost revenue.

Erie Street and the downtown core are home to some of Ontario’s finest restaurants, and with that culinary excellence comes cockroach risk. German cockroaches thrive in the warm, humid conditions of commercial kitchens and can spread to neighbouring businesses through shared walls, plumbing, and utility chases. Our discreet commercial pest management programs keep Stratford’s restaurants compliant with health regulations and free of pest sightings year-round.

Stratford’s residential neighbourhoods — from the grand Victorian homes near the Avon River to the East End and West End — face their own challenges. Century-old brick construction with stone foundations, ornamental woodwork, and aging mortar provides rodents and carpenter ants with abundant entry points. Mice in particular exploit gaps around original window frames, foundation settling cracks, and deteriorating weep holes to access wall cavities and basements during colder months.
