Our Expertise

Every Pest. One Call. 14 Solutions.

Each treatment is tailored to the specific pest, your property type, and your local conditions. Every service includes a free inspection and written guarantee.

🐛

Bed Bug Removal

Professional heat treatment and targeted chemical sprays eliminate bed bugs at every life stage. 90-day guarantee included with every treatment.

→ 🐭

Rodent Control

Complete mouse and rat removal with full exclusion. We seal every entry point and set up monitoring stations to keep them out permanently.

→ 🐜

Ant Extermination

Carpenter ants, pavement ants, and pharaoh ants all require different approaches. We identify the species and destroy the entire colony at its source.

→ 🪫

Cockroach Control

German cockroaches, Oriental cockroaches, and more. Our gel bait and residual spray program wipes out infestations within 2–3 weeks.

→ 🦝

Wildlife Removal

Humane raccoon, skunk, and squirrel eviction following Ontario wildlife regulations. Includes exclusion repairs and a 2-year re-entry warranty.

→ 🪵

Termite Control

Subterranean termite detection and elimination using liquid barrier treatments and monitoring stations. Protect your home's structural integrity.

→ 🐝

Wasp & Hornet Removal

Safe removal of wasp nests, yellow jacket colonies, and hornet nests from eaves, soffits, and underground burrows. Same-day emergency service available.

→ 🕷

Tick Control

Perimeter tick treatments for residential properties. We target blacklegged ticks that carry Lyme disease, protecting your family and pets outdoors.

→ 🦋

Moth Control

Pantry moths and clothes moths damage your food and fabrics. Our targeted treatment eliminates larvae and adults, with prevention tips to stop recurrence.

→ 🕸

Spider Control

Residual barrier treatments along baseboards, window frames, and exterior entry points reduce spider populations and keep them from coming back inside.

→ 🦟

Flea Control

Full-home flea treatment targeting carpets, furniture, and pet resting areas. Our two-visit protocol breaks the flea life cycle completely.

→ 🐟

Silverfish Control

Silverfish thrive in damp basements and bathrooms. We treat harbourage areas, reduce moisture attractants, and apply residual products for long-term control.

→ 🪮

Earwig Control

Earwigs invade through cracks and gaps in foundations. Our perimeter treatment and interior application eliminates current populations and deters new ones.

→ 🏢

Commercial Pest Control

Restaurants, warehouses, offices, and retail spaces. We provide Integrated Pest Management plans, documentation for health inspections, and discreet service.
